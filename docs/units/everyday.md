### Conversion Notes

- Higher mpg = better fuel economy
- Lower L/100km = better fuel economy
- US gallon ≠ Imperial gallon (3.785 L vs 4.546 L)
- 1 mpg (US) ≈ 1.201 mpg (Imperial)
