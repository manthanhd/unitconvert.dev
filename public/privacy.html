Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 6, 2024

Overview

Unit Converter (unitconvert.dev) is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data and respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What Data We Collect

We collect very little information:

  • No personal information: We don't collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No user accounts: The site doesn't require registration or login.
  • Anonymous analytics: Basic usage statistics through Plausible Analytics (see below).
  • Local storage only: Your recent conversions are stored locally in your browser.

Analytics (Plausible)

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics service that:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track you across websites
  • Is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR
  • Collects only anonymous page views and basic usage statistics

All analytics data is aggregated and anonymous. We cannot identify individual users. Learn more about Plausible's privacy practices at plausible.io/privacy.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your recent conversions. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings
  • Improves your experience by remembering recent conversions

To clear this data, use your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature or press Cmd/Ctrl+K in the app to reset.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. The site functions entirely without cookies, and we don't use any third-party services that set cookies.

Third-Party Services

We only use one third-party service:

  • Plausible Analytics: For privacy-friendly, anonymous usage statistics. See the Analytics section above for details.

We do not use any other tracking, advertising, or analytics services.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties. Since we don't collect personal information, there's nothing to share.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal data, you have nothing to request, delete, or export. However:

  • You can clear your local conversion history at any time using your browser settings
  • You can block Plausible Analytics using browser extensions or privacy tools
  • The entire site works without JavaScript if you prefer

Children's Privacy

Our service is available to users of all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the information available at unitconvert.dev.
